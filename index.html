📖 About Our Legacy

27 Years of Educational Excellence

Lucy Education Center was founded 27 years ago in 1991. It is the oldest learning center in our city. Until now, it is a competitive and reputable school, which has produced great people. It currently has a good name and reputation in our city in terms of learning and teaching.

Our Vision

As the historical Lucy "Dinknesh" was discovered in Ethiopia and became well known throughout the world, our vision is to see our school and our students competent in Ethiopia and all over the world.
